Visually, we see the same story as the numeric summary. It appears that HIP has higher foam Index than BM and IT. We can also see that there appear to be differences in the variances between groups.

Question 4

expresso.anova <- aov(foamIndx ~ method, data=expresso)
autoplot(expresso.anova)

Based on the Residuals vs Fitted and Scale-Location plot overall we have reasonably constant variance. Normality assumption also looks reasonable.
